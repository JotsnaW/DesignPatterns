Problem statement -
You have a Laptop with three pin plug and which support three pin outlet configuration.
Suppose, you are travelling to a country where they support two pin outlet configuration.
Now both outlet uses different interfaces. How will you connect your laptop plug to the outlet. 

Solution statement - 
TO bridge gap between your laptop plug and power outlet, we can use plug Adapter.
Here Plug Adapter will serve as an Adapter between your laptop's plug and the power outlet, 
allowing you to connect your laptop to power source without needing to change the laptop's plug or the power outlet itself.
